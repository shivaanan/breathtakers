Breathtakers

About Us

The go-to tracking app to help curb smoking.

Breathtakers is a student-developed website that aims to motivate current smokers to stop smoking through distractions and informative news that sway them away from their unhealthy habits.

This website contains a tracker to count non-smoking days, games that divert users attention from smoking, a news page that features smoking articles, and finally, an events page where users can sign up for activites to distract themselves from puffing.

Sign up by clicking the signup button above to access these features!
If you are an existing user, click on the login button above to log in to your account.

We hope that this website can benefit those that require help, and even those who prefer a discreet means of helping oneself.
